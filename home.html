About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel Petro. I am a Front-End Web Developer with a primary focus on UX/UI Design. I've always enjoyed the concept of design. Being able to figure out the underlying logic and reasoning behind an aesthetically pleasing design gets me excited. Whether it be a website, a logo, or product.

Career Aspirations

Designer

Designing became a passion that I never knew I had until recently. Looking back at life thus far designing has played a huge role in who I am and who I want to become.

Ever since I was little designing my own Kinex roller coasters and drawing random logos, mimmicking muscians album artwork in school. Designing my baseball and soccer teams fundraiser apparel, to selling t-shirts in college at football games, all the way to designing my current companies product label design.
